- **Native Experience**: Desktop app feel on desktop, native mobile app feel on mobile

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Identity
- **Primary Colors**: Elegant gold/beige palette (#D4AF37, #C9A961, #B8956A) - warm, sophisticated
- **Inspiration**: Netflix, Disney+, Apple TV+, Apple Books, Medium
- **Typography**: Inter (English), Vazirmatn (Persian) with proper RTL support
- **Dark Mode**: Fully supported with smooth transitions, optimized for reading

### UX Principles
- **Performance First**: Lighthouse 90+, < 2s load time, optimized images
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first, tablet-optimized, desktop-enhanced
- **Accessibility**: WCAG compliant, keyboard navigation, screen reader support
- **Micro-interactions**: Satisfying hover effects, smooth transitions (200-500ms)
- **Loading States**: Skeleton screens (not spinners), progressive loading
- **Error Handling**: Graceful degradation, helpful error messages

### Animation Standards
- Page transitions: 300-500ms cubic-bezier
- Micro-interactions: 150-250ms ease-out
- Page turns: 600-800ms with realistic physics
- Hover effects: 200ms ease-in-out
- Scroll-triggered: Intersection Observer with stagger

## Target Audience

### Primary Users
- **English Learners**: Persian speakers learning English (beginner to advanced)
- **Book Enthusiasts**: Readers who want bilingual support
- **Students**: Academic English learners, exam preparation

### User Personas
1. **Beginner Learner**: Needs translations, simple vocabulary, slow pace
2. **Intermediate Reader**: Wants contextual help, vocabulary building, moderate challenge
3. **Advanced User**: Seeks authentic content, minimal assistance, speed reading
